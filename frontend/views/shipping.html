Shipping Policy

We ship across Bosnia and Herzegovina within 2-5 business days. Orders placed before 15:00 are processed the same day (weekdays).

  • Standard delivery: 2-5 business days
  • Same-city delivery (Sarajevo): next business day
  • Shipping fee: 7,00 BAM (free over 200,00 BAM)

You'll receive a confirmation email with tracking once your order is packed.
